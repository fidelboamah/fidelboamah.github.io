About

Designing clarity into complex data.

I'm a Senior Product Designer based in Atlanta. For the past 7+ years I've designed complex B2B ecosystems — CRM, customer data platforms, and AI-driven insights — most recently at Klaviyo, where I led design for AI Insights and core data management tools.

My work lives at the intersection of dense data and human decisions. The products I'm proudest of take something marketers used to spend days untangling and turn it into a clear next step they can trust.

How I Work

I design for trust first. AI products fail when people can't see why the system is recommending something — so I lead with evidence: confidence scores, explainability, and revenue framing that connects every suggestion to a business outcome. If a user can't validate it, they won't act on it.

I speak the language of the business. Before pitching the Profiles redesign, I partnered with analysts to put a dollar figure on design friction — and that number won the scope. The strongest design arguments are the ones leadership can take to a spreadsheet.

I think in systems, not screens. At Klaviyo I contributed 12 components to the design system and architected an AI-adaptive UI framework that doubled engineering delivery speed. My best work happens embedded with engineering and PM, not handing off specs from a distance.
